About us

What is it?
Moodle is a resource to help the Founders Academy Tribe #1 live better and make daily gratitude cool.

Where did it come from?
Adam and Antonia met on a spiritual retreat on High Street Ken back in December 2019, and bonded over their love of the Five Minute Journal and daily gratitude practices. After much back and forth, mainly over building the website, they kick started Moodle in February 2020. To date, Moodle has helped one person change their life.

It’s almost certain Moodle will help almost ten people soon.
